info About EDU.i

Built for Modern Campuses

EDU.i is an AI-powered smart campus platform designed to digitize and automate the traditional attendance management process. By leveraging facial recognition technology, EDU.i eliminates proxy attendance, reduces manual workload, and provides actionable insights through real-time analytics.

The platform also includes a comprehensive grievance reporting system, enabling students to voice their concerns — anonymously if needed — about academic, safety, or facility issues.
